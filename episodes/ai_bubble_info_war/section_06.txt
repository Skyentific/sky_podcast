OpenAI's 15 billion, 35 billion, or 110 billion dollar Round. Stop Reporting That It Raised 110 billion, It Is Factually Incorrect.

Great news! If you don't think about it for a second, OpenAI raised 110 billion dollars, with 50 billion from Amazon, 30 billion from NVIDIA and 30 billion from SoftBank. Well, okay, not really.

Per The Information: OpenAI raised 15 billion dollars from Amazon, with 35 billion contingent on AGI or an IPO. OpenAI got commitments from SoftBank and NVIDIA, who may or may not have committed to 30 billion each, and will be paying in three installments.

As of right now, evidence suggests that nobody has actually sent OpenAI any money.
